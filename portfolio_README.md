## Our Work

### 1. Financial Transaction Analytics
**Domain:** Fintech
**Data:** 3 tables + MCC codes | 13M+ transactions across 2010-2019
**Tools:** Python, DuckDB, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

End-to-end analytics on a fintech company's transaction data across customers,
cards, and transaction logs. Eight KPI modules covering channel performance,
error analysis, customer segmentation, merchant category analysis, card portfolio
performance, and timing patterns.

Key findings: Online channel carries the highest error rate at 2.2% despite
the highest average ticket value at $58. Insufficient Balance drives over 70%
of all transaction errors and $1M in affected value. Lower-Mid income band
drives $45M in spend — more than three times the next highest segment.

[View Project](https://github.com/datawellconsultancy/fintech-transaction-analytics)

### 2. Anomaly Detection and Transaction Monitoring
**Domain:** Fintech
**Data:** 1M+ daily transaction records across 10 years
**Tools:** Python, DuckDB, Pandas, Matplotlib

Automated anomaly detection system monitoring daily transaction count, volume,
and error rate using a 7-day rolling average with 2 standard deviation control
bands. Generates a prioritised alert report ranking every flagged day by
deviation percentage.

Key findings: Identified two complete platform outages on February 29, 2012
and 2016 at -99.6% and -99.1% deviation — a leap day date handling bug recurring
every 4 years. 86 total alerts across 83 unique dates. Error rate spikes dominate
with top deviation at 128.6% above rolling mean.

[View Project](https://github.com/datawellconsultancy/anomaly-detection-monitoring)

### 3. Healthcare Claims Analytics
**Domain:** Healthcare Insurance
**Data:** 4 tables | 40,000+ records across claims, providers, patients, payments
**Tools:** Python, DuckDB, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Full claims analytics for a healthcare insurer across four connected data sources.
Eight KPI modules covering claim approval rates, fraud pattern analysis by specialty
and insurance type, provider performance, payment settlement, patient demographics,
and yearly fraud trends.

Key findings: Pediatrics highest fraud rate at 11%, Cardiology highest volume
combined with elevated fraud — highest priority for intervention. All payment
methods average 60 days to settlement — bottleneck is upstream in approval process.
Fraud rate rising from 9.2% in 2021 to 10.3% in 2024.

[View Project](https://github.com/datawellconsultancy/healthcare-claims-analytics)

### 4. Fintech Servicing Operations Analytics
**Domain:** Fintech — Customer Support Operations
**Data:** 4 tables | 35,000+ records across tickets, agents, customers, interactions
**Tools:** Python, DuckDB, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Multi-channel support operations analytics across six channels — inbound calls,
outbound calls, live chat, email, chatbot, and social media. Eight KPI modules
covering SLA compliance, channel performance, issue type analysis, agent team
performance, chatbot effectiveness, and customer segment analysis.

Key findings: SLA breach rate uniform at 22% across all priority levels —
Critical tickets treated same as Low priority, confirming a process not capacity
problem. Chatbot transferring 42% of interactions to human agents — functioning
as router not resolver. SLA breach rate rising from 21.2% in 2022 to 22.5% in
2024 on completely flat ticket volume.

[View Project](https://github.com/datawellconsultancy/servicing-operations-analytics)
